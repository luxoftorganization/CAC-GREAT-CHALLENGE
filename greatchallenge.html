Welcome to Great Challenge Evangelical Bible College

Are you passionate about deepening your understanding of the Word of God? Do you desire to become a trained Bible scholar or enhance your knowledge in Church Management?

At Great Challenge Evangelical Bible College (G.C.E. Bible College), we are dedicated to providing sound biblical and theological education that prepares men and women for effective Christian service and leadership.

Our Programmes

We offer Two-Year Diploma and Certificate Programmes available in both Full-Time and Part-Time study options.

  • Missionary and Evangelism
  • Church Growth and Management

Our curriculum is designed to combine spiritual formation with academic excellence, equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to impact their churches, communities, and the world.

Why Choose G.C.E. Bible College?

  • Conducive learning environment
  • Flexible study options (Full-Time and Part-Time)
  • Affordable tuition fees
